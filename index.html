About me

Hi! I'm Chris. I've been learning full-stack web development while working full time as a pharmacy technician. I become interested in web development and programming as a whole after taking a career aptitude test. This was taken after deciding my current job at the time (special education para-professional) wasn't the best fit. I learned a lot in that role, and I will always be thankful for my experiences as well as the people I met—big and small— I decided I wanted to take on a new challenge, and discovered the world of web development and programming. I've had many ups and downs in my learning journey, and I considered giving up on many occasions, but I am still pushing, and creating this project as well as joining a coding bootcamp was a big step in the process. If you're reading this now, you're lucky to get a glimpse at takeoff. It's only up from here!
